Searching "carpenter near me" in Indore throws up dozens of results — but how do you pick the right one?

## 5 Things to Check Before Hiring

1. **Do they offer a free site visit?** A good carpenter or furniture contractor will come to you, measure correctly, and give an honest estimate — for free.
2. **Do they give a written warranty?** Verbal promises fade. Ask for a written warranty on workmanship.
3. **Can they show you past work?** Real photos of kitchens, wardrobes, and furniture they've built in Indore — not stock images.
4. **Do they handle the whole project?** Or do you need to separately hire a painter, plumber, and electrician? A furniture contractor manages the full scope.
5. **Are they local and responsive?** Someone who knows Indore homes — the typical flat sizes, the heat, the local materials — will do better work.
